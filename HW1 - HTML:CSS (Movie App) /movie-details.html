Title: Kung Fu Panda


Description: Po might just be the laziest, clumsiest panda in the Valley of Peace, but he secretly dreams of becoming a kung fu legend. When the villainous snow leopard Tai Lung threatens Po's homeland, the hapless panda is chosen to fulfil an ancient prophecy and defend the Valley from attack. Training under Master Shifu, Po embarks on an epic high-kicking adventure as he sets out to thwart Tai Lung's evil plans.


Directors: John Stevenson, Mark Osborne


Release Year: 2008


Budget: $130 million


Runtime: 92 minutes


Rating: PG


Genre: Children's Comedy/Action
